Recordings

During the recordings, all the magic happens, what starts as a vision turns into something real. Each take, every note, and the shared energy bring
it all together, turning ideas into pure sound. Listening to the song after
you finished the whole recording process is a blast to the soul, you can sense every musical note and creativity that went into making it real.
I would call it a surreal moment, and a reminder of why you fell in love
with making music in the first place.
